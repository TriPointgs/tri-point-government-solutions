# Brand Notes

## Brand direction

Tri-Point should feel:

- credible
- calm
- organized
- federal-contracting friendly
- small-business responsive

## Colors

- Navy: `#0B1F33`
- Federal Blue: `#1E3A5F`
- Muted Gold: `#C89B3C`
- Warm Cream: `#F7F3EA`
- Slate Text: `#5B6875`

## Logo idea

The mark uses a three-point shield concept:

- three points = Tri-Point
- shield = reliability and government/facility support
- gold outline = premium/professional accent
- navy base = serious federal contracting look

## Suggested tagline options

1. Reliable Solutions for Mission-Driven Buyers
2. Procurement • Logistics • Facility Support
3. Responsive Support for Government Requirements
4. Practical Solutions. Clear Communication. Reliable Follow-Through.
